Our Sun

The sun is an inexhaustible resource which when combined with the latest technology, offers economical and environmentally conscious solutions.

Solar Energy System

The solar panels contain photo-voltaic cells that capture the sun light to use as electricity for your home. The inverter then converts sun light from direct current (DC) to alternating current (AC), which is the same type of energy that can be used to power your home.

 

Your Home Powered by the Sun

With this system you will obtain the same functionality of electricity for your home. The only difference is that solar energy is much more friendly to the environment than traditional energy and by switching to solar you save money.

Utility Metering

Your energy consumption is measured by the utility meter. When the solar panels produce more electricity than is needed during peak sun hours, excess electricity is automatically sent to the grid (utility company) and the electric meter actually spins backwards, building a bank of credits at the utility company.

 

Power Grid

Interconnection system allow consumers to generate some or all of their own electricity. With this technology, the surplus energy is sent to the power grid which translates to a credit in the customers utility account, thus allowing them to use the power generated during the day at night.
